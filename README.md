Analysis: Zomato Ltd (NSE: ZOMATO)
Overview
Completed a fundamental analysis of Zomato, focusing on its transition from a high-burn startup to a profitable entity.

Key Projections (FY24 - FY26E)
Revenue Growth: Projecting a 40% YoY growth for FY26 as the Quick Commerce (Blinkit) segment continues to scale.

Margin Expansion: Estimated EBITDA Margin improvement from 8.47% (Mar-25) to 9.50% (Mar-26E) due to operating leverage.

Earnings Power: Forecasted EPS to reach ₹0.71 by March 2026.
